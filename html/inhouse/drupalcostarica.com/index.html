Why Drupal?

Drupal is a modern powerful content management system that can be used to power websites, apps and much more.

Community powered

Free to use and improve. Backed up by thousands of developers and companies around the globe.

Battle tested

Drupal has been used in a number of industries and website types, making it a safe bet.

Lots of addon modules

Besides the huge out of the box functionality, there are thousands of extra functionalities that can be added. Custom improvements and expansions are possible too. This makes Drupal a flexible and extensible tool.

Why Costa Rica?

Besides being able to have your dream vacations and visiting your outsource in the same trip, Costa Rica Drupal Community has several advantages for your project.

Location

Costa Rica is GMT-6, just in the middle of US timezones! Flights are 5-6 hours from the US and

Tech Level

Costa Rica has top level talent. This is why it is home for offices from giants like Publicis Groupe, Accenture and Wunderman-Thompson.

Service DNA

As a small country with little mining/oil resources, the country invested on education. Intel, PG, HP, Amazon (et al) have offices in Costa Rica.

Culture

Through media and cultural exchange, there is very low to none cultural barrier.

English Spoken

English is taught since pre school and most Development companies are fully English spoken.

Pura Vida

Means Pure Life. An easy going approach to life.
